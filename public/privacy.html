Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Neighborhood Krew (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you visit neighborhoodkrew.com or contact us by phone, form, or other methods.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information you provide (e.g., name, phone number, email, address, move details, message content).
  • Call information when you call or text us (e.g., call time/date, duration, and call recordings if enabled).
  • Website usage data (e.g., pages viewed, clicks, approximate location, device/browser information).
  • Advertising and attribution data (e.g., which ad, keyword, or campaign led to a visit or call).

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Respond to requests and provide quotes, scheduling, and customer support.
  • Operate and improve our website and services.
  • Measure marketing performance (for example, which ads generate calls or leads).
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Cookies, Analytics, and Call Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, and advertising measurement. Depending on your configuration, the site may use tools such as:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) to understand website usage and improve performance.
  • Google Ads to measure ad performance and conversions.
  • Call tracking (e.g., CallRail) to attribute calls/leads to marketing sources and improve service.

These providers may set cookies or collect information about your device and interactions with the site. You can limit cookies through your browser settings. Note that some features may not work properly without cookies.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers who help us run the business (e.g., hosting, analytics, call tracking, CRM, advertising).
  • For legal reasons if required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights and safety.
  • With your consent or at your direction.

Call Recording

Calls may be recorded for quality assurance, training, and recordkeeping where enabled and permitted by law. If you do not want a call recorded, please let us know and we can offer an alternative method to communicate when feasible.

Data Retention

We retain information as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, including providing services, maintaining business records, resolving disputes, and complying with legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • Cookies: Control cookies via browser settings.
  • Marketing: You can request we stop contacting you for marketing purposes.
  • Access/Deletion Requests: You may request access to or deletion of your information, subject to legal requirements.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
